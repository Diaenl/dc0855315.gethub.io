DIAENL the Person

My name is Daniel Clarke and I enjoy designing things and creating concepts for books, videos and games. Of all the crazy things I've been up to has all in part to satisfy my desire to bring my ideas to fruition. I thank you for indulging in them.

I am currently working on various projects from common designing elements for single use, to entire stories that I wish to see become a full length videos, prefferably animated rather than live-action. Of the main ticket items, one stands above the others called Generations. If you're a fan of Scott Westerfeld's Leviathon Series or Marie Lu's Legend series, I hope that you will enjoy this work as well.
