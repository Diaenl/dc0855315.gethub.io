Project One
Generations

This story follows two children from different worlds find themselves working together in a plan decades in the making by their parents to create future that won't lead to the end of humanity. Himari, a girl born a Child of Eden (Cellams next stage of human evolution genome) follows her mother into rebellion after the abuse of powers by the ruling Eden group and is forced to flee when it fails. Kobe, a boy found abandoned by a refuge that would became head of Machina'super soldier project to go along with the others, was raised in the crucible of virtual hellish realities to create soldiers with lifetims worth of knowledge and experience. The two meet when a secret research facility sends a distress call. Inside the two find themselves caught in an ambush by a new species of biological monstrosity.
